What I do

With a background in marketing and over five years working in arts and culture, I offer a range of services tailored to creative organisations, galleries and independent artists who want to communicate with clarity and intention.

Arts Marketing
i.
Multi-Channel Digital Strategies for Exhibitions, Projects & Events

Developing and executing integrated marketing plans utilising a variety of platforms (social media, digital channels, print, paid ads) to deepen audience engagement.

ii.
Content Creation

Creating and producing engaging content across all social media channels — from image and video editing to full video briefing, art direction and storytelling.

iii.
Copywriting & Editing

Writing and editing compelling copy tailored to each platform — from social media captions to press releases, website and paid ads.

iv.
Web & E-commerce Management

Maintaining artist websites and digital stores to ensure a seamless professional presence and sales pipeline.

v.
Partnerships & Collaborations

Manage partnerships with brands, influencers and the press to help you reach the right audiences.

Arts Management
vi.
Comprehensive Studio Administration & Sales Support

Managing complex diaries and serving as the primary liaison for galleries, museums and private collectors. Overseeing bookkeeping, expense tracking and invoicing, alongside professional sales presentations and client follow-up.

vii.
Archival & Database Oversight

Building and maintaining rigorous digital artwork inventories and physical archives to protect and track creative legacies.

viii.
Strategic Opportunity Research

Identifying and managing applications for grants, residencies and fundraising initiatives to advance artistic careers.
